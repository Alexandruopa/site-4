Corporate Identity

GAMES SANTIAGO JORGE is a multi-disciplinary enterprise headquartered in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Founded and led by our Key Principal, Santiago Jorge Games, our firm carries his family name as a hallmark of quality, deeply rooted in the agricultural and engineering sectors.

We operate at the intersection of infrastructure and agronomy. Our expertise spans Architectural and Engineering Services, large-scale Oilseed and Grain Farming, and modern Cattle Ranching. By combining traditional farming with modern engineering, we ensure maximum yield and sustainability.

GSJ AgTech Connect App

To unify our engineering blueprints and farming operations, GAMES SANTIAGO JORGE has developed a proprietary B2B mobile application published on the Google Play Store.

The GSJ AgTech Connect app is a powerful tool for farm managers, architects, and agricultural engineers. It digitizes cattle tracking, monitors crop yields, and provides secure mobile access to structural engineering blueprints.

  • IoT Cattle Tracking: Monitor herd locations and health stats via GPS.
  • Crop Management: Access real-time data for oilseed and grain yields.
  • Digital Blueprints: Secure mobile access to architectural site plans.
  • B2B Contractor Portal: Streamlined communication for engineering services.
